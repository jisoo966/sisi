# Sísí — User Interview Guide

*Created: 2026년 5월 13일*
*Goal: 5-7명 인터뷰 (30-45분 each), Brand DNA + MVP scope 검증*

---

## 🎯 인터뷰 목표 (왜 하나)

이 인터뷰의 진짜 목표는 *"feature ideas 얻기"* 가 아니야. **본인 brand DNA + product 가정이 맞는지 검증**.

### 검증해야 할 5가지 가설

1. **Manifestation 사용자는 *cringy* 한 앱에 진짜로 거부감 있나?**
   → 본인 anti-woo positioning이 맞는지

2. **Vision board를 락스크린에 두는 것 = 진짜 가치 있나?**
   → MVP 킬러 기능 검증

3. **Synchronicity Timeline (*"이번 주 끌어당긴 것"*) = 사용자가 진짜 원하나?**
   → Retention 무기 검증

4. **"AI" 단어 빼고 *"friend"* 포지셔닝 = 사용자한테 felt 다른가?**
   → Brand voice 검증

5. **월 $9.99 = 사용자가 낼 의향 있나?**
   → 가격 검증

이 5가지에 *yes·no·partial yes* 답을 받는 게 인터뷰의 진짜 결과물.

## 📋 Interview Script (30-45분)

### 사전 준비
- [ ] Zoom 또는 Google Meet 셋업
- [ ] 녹음 허락 받기 (intro에서)
- [ ] 노트북·휴대폰 2화면 (인터뷰 보면서 동시 노트)
- [ ] hellosisi.co 페이지 share 준비 (인터뷰 후반)
- [ ] 빠른 노트 템플릿 (구글 닥)

### Opening (2분) — Set up trust

```
"Hi [name]! Thanks so much for jumping on. Quick context:

I'm building Sísí — kind of an inner self companion app for 
manifestation. Before I build the wrong thing, I'm interviewing 
7 people who actually use manifestation. 

This isn't a sales call — I genuinely want to learn how YOU 
manifest. There's no wrong answer.

I'd love to record this so I can focus on listening — that okay 
with you? It's only for my notes.

Any questions before we start?"
```

### Section 1: Background (8분) — How they manifest

**핵심 질문:**

1. *"Walk me through how you got into manifestation. When did it start?"*
   - 흘려보내. 그들의 *first encounter* 들으면 trigger·motivation 알 수 있음.

2. *"How would you describe your manifestation practice today? Like, what does a typical week look like?"*
   - *Daily? Weekly? Sporadic?* 빈도 알아내기

3. *"What does manifestation actually mean to you?"*
   - 그들의 *philosophy* 캐치 (Goddard? Hicks? casual?)

**관찰 포인트:**
- 어떤 단어 자주 씀? (*"vibrate", "align", "calling in", "trust" 등*)
- 영성·neuroscience·tech 어느 결?
- *"진짜 믿음"* vs *"playful belief"* 어디?

### Section 2: Tools & Methods (10분) — Current behavior

**핵심 질문:**

4. *"What apps or tools do you currently use for manifestation?"*
   - 답한 앱 다 적기 (I am? Stella? Co-Star? Notion? journal?)

5. *"Walk me through what works in [most-mentioned app]."*
   - 그들이 *love* 하는 부분 알아내기

6. *"What doesn't work? What frustrates you?"*
   - 진짜 pain points

7. *"Do you have a vision board? How do you maintain it?"*
   - 본인 핵심 기능 검증

8. *"Have you ever tracked manifestations over time — like, what you've called in?"*
   - Synchronicity Timeline 검증 (대부분 *"no, but I wish"* 답할 거)

9. *"What's the biggest gap between what you do and what would actually help?"*
   - **이 답이 골드**. MVP 진짜 needs

### Section 3: Pain points deeper dive (8분) — Emotional layer

10. *"Have you ever quit a manifestation practice? Why?"*
    - 본인 *"streak 압박 X"* 포지셔닝 검증

11. *"What about manifestation apps feels cringy or off-putting?"*
    - 본인 anti-woo positioning 검증 (이게 진짜 가설)

12. *"Has anyone in your life been skeptical of manifestation? What did they say?"*
    - 회의주의자 진입 모먼트 탐구

13. *"What would a manifestation app for skeptical people look like?"*
    - 본인 positioning 검증

14. *"Are you ever embarrassed to talk about manifestation? With whom?"*
    - 진짜 emotional layer

### Section 4: Sísí Show (10분) — Reactions

**Show hellosisi.co landing page. 침묵하고 그들 reaction.**

15. *"What's your first impression? Just honest reaction."*

16. *"What do you think this is?"* (positioning understood?)

17. *"What does 'your inner self friend' make you think?"* (tagline)

18. *"What would you expect this app to do?"* (feature gap?)

19. *"On a scale 1-10, how interested would you be in trying this?"* 
    - 1-3: 왜 안 끌리나?
    - 4-7: 무엇이 더 있어야 끌리나?
    - 8-10: 무엇이 가장 끌리나?

20. *"What's missing? What would make you a 10?"*

### Section 5: Pricing & Beta (5분) — Validation

21. *"If this existed and worked beautifully, what would you pay monthly?"*
    - 5? 10? 15? 30?

22. *"Would you pay for premium features like daily Sísí voice messages, 
    longer synchronicity history, or AI vision board generation?"*

23. *"If I built a beta in 60 days, would you want to be a tester?"*

24. *"Can I follow up in 2-4 weeks with the early version?"*

### Closing (2분)

```
"This was so helpful — thank you. A few last things:

1. Anyone else you know who'd be great to talk to?

2. I'll send you the beta first — 60 days. Sound good?

3. Anything else you want to add about manifestation that we 
   didn't cover?"
```

### 패턴 찾기 질문

2. **5명 이상이 같은 pain point 말한 게 뭐야?** → MVP 우선 기능
3. **5명 이상이 같은 앱 칭찬한 게 뭐야?** → 본인이 borrow할 거
4. **본인이 가장 놀란 인사이트는?** → Brand DNA·MVP에 반영
5. **무엇이 본인 가설을 *반박*했어?** → 정직하게 pivot

## 💡 인터뷰 진행 팁

1. **80% 듣고 20% 말해.** 사용자가 70% 시간 말해야 함.

2. **답변에 추가 질문 *"왜 그게 중요해?"*** — 5번 *"왜"* 물으면 진짜 motivation 나옴 (5 Whys).

3. **본인 의견·미래 기능 PUSH X.** *"우리는 이거 만들 거야, 어때?"* 묻지 마. 그들의 진짜 needs 먼저.

4. **"좋은 아이디어다"** 라고 말하지 마. *"흥미롭네"* 또는 그냥 *끄덕이기*.

5. **5초 침묵 견디기.** 가장 깊은 답은 침묵 후 나옴.

6. **녹음하면서 노트.** 후에 다시 들으면 놓친 거 발견.

7. **첫 1-2명은 friend로.** 본인이 인터뷰 *flow* 익숙해진 후 Reddit 사람 인터뷰.
