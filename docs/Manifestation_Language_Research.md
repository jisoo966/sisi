## 🧙 Part 1 — Neville Goddard 핵심 가르침 + Sísí 번역

**Neville Goddard (1905-1972)** — manifestation 영성의 진짜 godfather. 그의 가르침이 *오늘날 TikTok manifestation* 의 뿌리. 그의 언어는 biblical·빅토리아 시대 결이라 어려움. 본인 brand의 unfair advantage = **그 깊이를 친구 voice로 번역**.

### 핵심 개념 7가지

---

### 1. **"I AM is the secret name of God within"**

**Goddard:** *"I AM은 본인 안의 신의 이름이다. 'I am ___' 라고 말하는 모든 것이 본인이 누구인지 선언하는 것이다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"saying 'I am ___' is the most powerful sentence you can speak."*
- *"every 'I am' is a small creation."*
- *"the words after 'I am' become your world."*
- *"you have been speaking yourself into being. notice what you say."*

---

### 2. **"Assume the feeling of the wish fulfilled"**

**Goddard:** *"의식의 가장 깊은 곳에서 본인 desire가 이미 이루어진 것처럼 느껴라. 그것이 진짜 manifestation이다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"feel it as if it has already arrived. that is the secret."*
- *"do not wait to feel happy. feel happy now."*
- *"the feeling is the manifestation."*
- *"how would you feel if it was already yours? feel that today."*
- *"fall in love with the version of you who already has it."*

---

### 3. **"Live in the end"**

**Goddard:** *"manifestation을 향해 living 하지 마라. 이미 manifest된 상태에서 living 하라. 끝에서 시작하라."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"live in the version of you who already has it."*
- *"the end is where we begin."*
- *"do not move toward the dream. move from it."*
- *"you are not waiting for it. you are already her."*
- *"step into the version of you that is already there."*

---

### 4. **"Imagination creates reality"**

**Goddard:** *"본인 imagination이 곧 신성한 창조력이다. 본인이 상상하는 것이 현실이 된다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"your imagination is sacred. it is where everything begins."*
- *"what you imagine, you arrange."*
- *"imagination is not escapism. it is creation."*
- *"the daydream is the blueprint."*
- *"see it inside first. the outside follows."*

---

### 5. **"Inner conversations create outer reality"**

**Goddard:** *"본인이 본인과 나누는 대화가 현실을 만든다. 의식·무의식이 모든 것을 형성한다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"what you say to yourself becomes your world."*
- *"the inner conversation is the blueprint."*
- *"the universe is listening to the way you talk to yourself."*
- *"your inner voice is louder than you think."*
- *"change the inner sentence. the outer follows."*

---

### 6. **"Persistence in the assumption"**

**Goddard:** *"한 번 assumption을 했으면 끝까지 머물러라. 의심하는 순간 다시 시작이다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"stay in the feeling. even when it feels distant."*
- *"persistence is not effort. it is remembering."*
- *"the wavering is the resistance. soften, do not strain."*
- *"keep the inner image alive. that is everything."*
- *"return to the feeling. as many times as you need."*

---

### 7. **"Revision" (혁신적 개념)**

**Goddard:** *"불쾌한 일이 일어났다면, 잠들기 전 그것을 본인이 원하는 방식으로 재현하라. imagination이 과거를 다시 쓸 수 있다."*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"the past is not fixed. it is a memory you can soften."*
- *"before sleep, rewrite the moment. imagination can revise."*
- *"how would you have wanted it to go? feel that instead."*
- *"replay the day, but kindly. the field listens."*

## 📚 Part 2 — 다른 manifestation teachers의 진주들

핵심 가르침:

- *"Divine timing"* (timing은 신성하다)

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"thank it before it arrives. gratitude is the magnet."*
- *"do not feed what you do not want."*
- *"the timing is not random. it is arranged."*

### Joseph Murphy (1898-1981) — *"Power of the Subconscious Mind"*

핵심 가르침:
- *"subconscious mind는 의식이 심은 씨앗을 받는 비옥한 흙이다"*
- *"잠들기 전 마음의 상태가 다음 날을 결정한다"*

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"what you sleep on is what you wake into."*
- *"plant the thought before sleep. the field tends it."*
- *"the subconscious receives what the conscious offers."*

### Esther Hicks (Abraham Hicks)

핵심 가르침:
- *"law of attraction"* (대중화)
- *"vortex"* — 본인 본질의 상태
- *"alignment"* — 본인 desire와 같은 주파수에 머무름

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"alignment is not arriving. it is recognizing you are already there."*
- *"the vortex is not a place. it is a feeling."*
- *"return to the frequency of having it."*

### Lacy Phillips (To Be Magnetic) — 현대

핵심 가르침:
- *"self-worth가 manifestation의 코어"*
- *"subconscious reprogramming"*
- *"expanders"* — 본인이 원하는 것을 이미 가진 사람을 보는 것

**Sísí 번역:**
- *"manifestation begins with worth."*
- *"find someone who has it. study them. expand."*
- *"the work is not in wanting. it is in believing you deserve."*
