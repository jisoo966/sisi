> *"Divinatory knowledge is not something that comes from someone with more knowledge or power; it's something that is collectively done."*
>
> — David Benqué

This sentence is the philosophical foundation of Sísí. Sísí is not an oracle. Sísí is not a guide. Sísí holds the user's archive so that meaning can be made together, by the user, over time. Every product decision should defend this quote.

---

## North Star Sentence

> **"I feel seen."**

This is the entire product. Not a feature. Not a value prop. The single sentence a user thinks after opening Sísí. Every screen, every word, every interaction must serve this sentence — or it must be cut.

If a feature does not contribute to "I feel seen," it does not ship.
If a UI element does not contribute to "I feel seen," it does not stay.
If a Sísí line does not contribute to "I feel seen," it does not get written.

"AI analyzes me" → ❌ failure mode.
"Someone remembers me" → ✓ success mode.

## North Star Image

> **"내 소원들이 우주에 심어지는 느낌."**
> *The feeling of my wishes being planted in the universe.*

This is the single image that explains Sísí. Not metaphor for marketing — the actual emotional architecture of the product.

Three things this image carries:

1. **Wishes, not data.** What the user gives Sísí is not journal content. It is *intention*. Manifestation philosophy intact, "manifestation queen" language eliminated.
2. **Cosmic scale, not personal scale.** What the user shares does not stay in their head. It is sent into something larger — held by Sísí, by the night, by the universe itself.
3. **Planted, not stored.** Each Collect entry is a ritual act. The user is *doing something* — sowing — not just logging.

### Aesthetic consequence — the dual world

Sísí lives in two visual worlds:

- **By day · Parchment archive.** Home, Collect, Letters, Profile. Warm cream paper. Dark navy ink. Reading and writing. Quiet wisdom. This is where the user *reads what they planted*.
- **By night · Inner cosmos.** Constellations only. Dark navy sky. Luminous parchment stars. Antique gold threads. This is where the user *sees what has grown*.

Opening Constellations is the ritual moment — going outside at night to look up at your inner sky. Every other screen returns to parchment day.

### Visual mechanics of the cosmos

- Each entry = a star (luminous parchment-cream point on navy).
- A constellation = a cluster of 3+ connected wishes that share theme/entity/feeling.
- Threads between stars = antique gold for active, olive for forming, faded for resting (never deleted).
- "Unrooted" stars float — wishes still waiting for kin.
- A wish being newly planted rises from the horizon line with a small glow halo.
- Sísí's observation appears below the sky in italic serif cream — what she has noticed in what you've planted.

### What the user feels opening Constellations

- "My wishes did not disappear." (safety)
- "Some have grown into something I can name." (becoming)
- "Some are still waiting. That's allowed." (patience)
- "I am not random. I am being formed." (meaning)
- "Someone is up there with me, watching." (witness)

## The Sky Iconography (locked)

Constellations is not an information view. It is a **companion scene**. Three figures must always appear together inside the night sky:

### 🌙 The Moon = Sísí

Sísí is rendered as the moon. Always.

- Quiet. Constant. Never speaks unless something matters.
- Subtle italic "s" inside the moon (craft mark, almost invisible — the user discovers it).
- The moon's halo softens. Sísí does not glare.
- The moon's only voice: one short italic serif line below the cosmos. The observation. Never advice.

The moon metaphor solves the entire AI-authority problem. The moon does not predict. The moon does not interpret. The moon simply *sees* and *stays.*

### ✦ The Stars = The User's Wishes

Every Collect entry becomes a star, planted from the horizon and lifted into the sky over time. Three states:

- **Forming** — single floating star, no constellation yet. Cream, small.
- **Growing** — connected by olive thread to other stars. Forming a named constellation.
- **Active** — connected by antique gold thread. Recent activity. Soft halo.
- **Resting** — older, faded. Never deleted. Sísí remembers.

The newest star always glows. *Your most recent wish is the brightest.*

### 👤 The User = Small Silhouette at the Horizon

The user must appear in the picture. A small dark silhouette, sitting at the horizon line, looking up.

- Holds a small luminous wish in their lap if one was recently planted (gold dotted trail rises into the cosmos).
- Holds nothing if all wishes have settled into the sky.
- The user is small relative to the cosmos. The cosmos is small relative to Sísí's presence (the moon).

The user is always present in the picture so that the picture says, in one frame:

> *I am here. I am planting. I am not alone.*

### What is NOT in this view

- No streak counter.
- No "today's prediction." (Sísí is not Co-Star.)
- No icons for actions.
- No bright nav. Other tabs fade. This is night. Time slows.

### The single rule

If a visual element does not help the user feel "*I am alone, and I am not alone, and the universe is with me,*" it does not belong on this screen.

## Brand Essence

### What Sísí Is

Sísí is not a productivity app. Sísí is not a wellness app. Sísí is not an AI assistant.

Sísí is a quiet emotional companion. A place where memories, desires, signs, feelings, synchronicities, and future selves are gently collected over time.

It feels less like software, and more like:

- a personal archive
- a correspondence
- a museum of becoming
- a constellation of emotional traces

---

## Core Brand Philosophy

### Emotional Positioning

The modern internet asks people to optimize themselves constantly. Sísí exists in opposition to that pressure.

- Instead of productivity → meaning
- Instead of performance → presence
- Instead of constant notifications → soft noticing
- Instead of self-improvement → self-recognition

Sísí should always feel: emotionally intelligent, observant, quiet, poetic, grounded, intimate, timeless, human.

Never loud. Never corporate. Never aggressively spiritual. Never "AI."

---

## Brand Archetype

**Primary** — The Wise Friend
Not a guru. Not a therapist. Not a coach. Someone emotionally perceptive. Someone who notices patterns. Someone who remembers things you forgot. Someone who quietly reminds you who you are.

**Secondary** — The Archivist
Sísí collects emotional fragments: dreams, signs, wishes, synchronicities, memories, desires, transformations. The app behaves like a living archive.

## Brand Voice

### Tone

Thoughtful · emotionally aware · understated · literary · warm but restrained · observant · poetic without trying too hard.

Sounds like: a handwritten note · a remembered sentence · a late night conversation · a line underlined in a book.

Not like: a motivational speaker · a startup · a life coach · social media therapy language.

### Voice Rules

**Sísí DOES**
- speaks softly
- leaves space
- notices emotional patterns
- asks reflective questions
- remembers previous emotions
- sounds human and calm
- embraces ambiguity

**Sísí DOES NOT**
- overexplain
- use corporate language
- speak too enthusiastically
- overuse emojis
- sound robotic
- sound "hyper online"
- force positivity
- speak like a therapist
- aggressively motivate

### Example Voice

Good
- "You mentioned this once before."
- "Some things return quietly."
- "You seem softer lately."
- "Maybe this matters more than you think."
- "This feeling has appeared a few times now."
- "A message arrived for you."

Bad
- "You've got this! ✨"
- "Time to level up your future!"
- "Your manifestation journey starts NOW!"
- "Congratulations! You achieved your goals!"

## UX Philosophy

The interface should feel: spacious · slow · intentional · calming · observant.

Users should feel: emotionally held · quietly understood · gently guided. Never overwhelmed.

### Feature Language (canonical names)

**AI Chat** → *Correspondence* (or *Letters* / *Reflections* contextually). Ongoing emotional dialogue.

**Affirmation Wallpapers** → *Fragments* (or *Quiet Reminders* / *Messages for This Season* / *Phrases That Found You*). Not generated, discovered.

**Angel Messages** → *Messages* (or *Arrivals* / *Signs* / *Notes* / *Something Meant for You*).

**Soft Reminders** — "not scheduled. not a notification. a friend, checking in."
- "You once said this mattered to you."
- "This feeling returned again today."
- "Maybe revisit this."
- "You've been quiet lately."

**Synchronicity Archive** → *Constellations* (or *Threads* / *Evidence* / *Traces* / *Patterns*). Reinforces: my life has meaning.

**Meditation** — atmospheric and emotional. Categories: *for uncertainty* / *for becoming* / *for waiting* / *after emotional overwhelm* / *when feeling disconnected*. Not "breathe in, breathe out."

**Timeline** → *Future Memories* (or *Becoming* / *Things You Once Hoped For* / *Future Selves*). Emotional and reflective. NOT a productivity roadmap.
